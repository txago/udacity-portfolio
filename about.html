About Me

I'm an UX designer & UI engineer passionate about building beautiful and intuitive solutions to users' needs. I have a strong background in visual design and I'm also a self-taught front-end developer. I have been continuously learning and improving my skills since I started out with web design in 1997.

My experience includes applying design approaches – from user research to prototyping, testing and implementing with code – along with Lean UX and Agile Methods.
